This site may display public notices related to device integrity, verification, and security review.

## Notice
Any device-related identifier, token, seal, label, or assertion presented here is informational unless independently verified through controlled infrastructure, cryptographic proof, or documented system authority.

## Safety rule
Do not rely on unauthenticated device notices as proof of authorization, ownership, or security status.

## Review standard
Questions regarding device authenticity, trust, or status should be resolved through verifiable logs, signed records, domain control, infrastructure control, and documented review.
